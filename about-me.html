Biography
Hi, I am Walter, an artist living in Fairfax, VA. I've always enjoyed creating art as much as I enjoy viewing them, and everything I create begin with two things: my passion and my drive.

Another fun fact about me is that I am fluent in three languages, namely: English, Mandarin, and Uyghur.
