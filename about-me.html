Biography
Hi, I am Yushan, an artist currently living in Richmond, VA. I've always enjoyed creating art as much as I enjoy viewing them, and everything I create begin with two things: my passion and my drive.

I am currently a student of VCUart under the department of Painting and Printmaking, class of 2021. My artworks have been published in Amendment Journal and Plain China at VCU. Here are some fun facts about me. I've used pseudonyms such as Logan Sullivan, Husan Yakup, and 丸手 in the past to sign my artworks. Another fun fact about me is that I am fluent in three languages, namely: English, Mandarin, and Uyghur.
